About Me

With over 19 years of experience in IT operations, I am Joslynn Smith, a Senior Consultant specializing in serving the Healthcare and Financial and Insurance services industries for government and non-profit healthcare organizations.

My main responsibilities include Business Analysis with some Product Owner duties, serving as a Team Lead (scrum master) for small development efforts, and handling MetaData Documentation (ERD, PDFs, and technical specifications). I also provide strategic consulting, and have experience creating a local DevOps team in response to a client's sudden need for a DataOps team.

I excel in technical and business project documentation, from requirements elicitation to system design and training documentation. Additionally, I specialize in agile methodologies, particularly as a scrum master for Kanban-style teams.

In my most recent experience, I worked with a growing cross-functional agile team, managing a complex list of requirements, confusing expectations, and an unclear vision. Assuming the role of a Business Analyst and undertaking some of the responsibilities of the Product Owner, I collaborated with the design and Scrum Master to ensure smooth requirements intake, resulting in an increased team velocity.

My focus lies in data-centric initiatives and issues, specifically in data governance, backbone integration, process and data movement, and data science and analytic insights.
